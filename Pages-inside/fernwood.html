Description

Fernwood Gardens is a premier garden events venue in Manila, known for its lush tropical landscapes and impeccable Filipino service. It is one of the most sought-after events venues in Manila, offering a picturesque setting for weddings, debuts, corporate events, and other special occasions. The venue features beautiful gardens, transparent dome-covered venues, and elegant architecture, creating a romantic and enchanting atmosphere. Fernwood Gardens provides a range of packages and services to ensure a memorable event experience.

History

The exact history of Fernwood Gardens is not readily available. However, it has established itself as a renowned garden events venue in Quezon City, Philippines. Over the years, Fernwood Gardens has gained a reputation for its stunning landscapes, excellent service, and the ability to create unforgettable moments for its clients. It has become a popular choice for couples looking for a dreamy garden wedding venue and individuals planning various special events.
