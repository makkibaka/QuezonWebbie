Description

Araneta City, formerly known as Araneta Center, is a 35-hectare (86-acre) transit-oriented, commercial mixed-use urban development located in Quezon City, Philippines. It provides unprecedented access to the North-South and East-West pathways of Metro Manila as it sits at the intersection of two important thoroughfares, EDSA and Aurora Boulevard. Araneta City offers a wide range of experiences, including shopping centers like Gateway Mall, Ali Mall, and Farmers Plaza, dining options, entertainment venues like the Smart Araneta Coliseum, and attractions such as the Art in Island 3D Museum. It is a vibrant hub that welcomes everyone.

History

Araneta City, known as the "City of Firsts," has a rich pioneering heritage. It has evolved from Araneta Center to Araneta City, but its soul remains the same. The development is owned, developed, and managed by ACI, Inc., which has transformed the area into a mixed-use hub of retail, entertainment, residential, hospitality, and office developments. With ongoing developments and exciting prospects for the future, Araneta City continues to grow and thrive as a dynamic urban destination.
